OSRTC Bus Services in Bhubaneswar

The Odisha State Road Transport Corporation (OSRTC) operates an extensive public transport network in Bhubaneswar, connecting key areas of the city and neighboring regions. With affordable fares and eco-friendly buses (including electric "Mo Buses"), OSRTC ensures convenient travel for residents and visitors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the OSRTC and bus route.

Use our Bhubaneswar Bus Route Finder tool by entering your source and destination. For real-time updates, check the OSRTC App or Google Maps.

OSRTC operates standard, AC, and electric "Mo Buses" for urban and intercity routes.

Tickets can be purchased on board, at bus terminals, or via the OSRTC mobile app.

Urban routes: Every 15-20 mins. Intercity routes: Hourly.

Limited night services are available until 10:00 PM for most routes.

Take OSRTC Route No. AIR-12 from the city center (e.g., Master Canteen) to Biju Patnaik International Airport (30 mins).

Bhubaneswar won the "City with the Best Public Transport System" award (2024) for its Mo Bus network and sustainable initiatives like electric buses.
