Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pune and PMPML bus routes.

Pune has 537 PMPML bus routes covering 7,715 stops across the city. Additionally, there are various types of bus services including Regular, Rainbow, Night, Pune Darshan, Airport, Ladies Special, and Air Conditioned Buses.

You can check Pune bus routes using the Moovit app or website, which provides real-time updates on bus times, schedules, and stops. Additionally, the PMPML website offers detailed route maps and schedules.

To find the route of a specific bus in Pune, you can use the Moovit app or visit the PMPML website. Enter the bus route number to get detailed information about the route, including stops and timings.

The longest PMPML bus route in Pune is not explicitly mentioned in the provided data. However, you can find this information by checking the detailed route maps on the PMPML website or using the Moovit app.

PMPML (Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Ltd) is the company that operates the public bus transport service in Pune. It runs multiple city buses between various destinations, connecting almost every part of Pune and its suburbs with its huge network of buses.

PMPML operates regular bus services connecting Pune to Mumbai, with buses departing every 30 minutes from Pune Station Bus Stand.
