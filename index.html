Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the BMTC and bus route.

You can use our Bangalore Bus Route Finder to search for the best BMTC bus routes by entering your source and destination. You can also check live updates using the Offcial BMTC App or Google Maps.

BMTC operates different types of buses, including:

  • Ordinary Buses – Budget-friendly travel
  • AC Vajra Buses – Comfortable, air-conditioned travel
  • Vayu Vajra Airport Buses – Direct airport connectivity
  • Electric Buses – Eco-friendly transport options

BMTC tickets are available on the bus itself. You can also buy daily, weekly, and monthly passes from BMTC counters or use the BMTC app for cashless payments.

Most BMTC buses run every 5–15 minutes on busy routes. However, timings may vary depending on the route, time of day, and traffic conditions.

Yes, some night buses (Night Service or Night Rider) operate on selected routes, especially between major transport hubs like Majestic, Airport, and IT corridors.

BMTC’s Vayu Vajra AC buses provide 24/7 direct connectivity to Kempegowda International Airport from different parts of the city.
