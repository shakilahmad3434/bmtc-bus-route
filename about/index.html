Our Story

Simplifying Bangalore's Bus Transport Since 2024

BangaloreCityBus.com was born out of a simple need - to make public transportation in Bangalore more accessible and user-friendly. We understand the challenges of navigating the city's extensive bus network and created this platform to help commuters plan their journeys efficiently.

Our team of transport enthusiasts and tech professionals has meticulously compiled bus routes, timetables, and schedules to create a comprehensive resource for daily commuters, occasional travelers, and visitors to Bangalore.

What started as a small project has now grown into a trusted information hub for thousands of commuters who rely on our accurate and up-to-date information to plan their daily travels.

Our Mission

Making Public Transport Accessible to All

At BangaloreCityBus.com, our mission is to promote the use of public transportation by making information easily accessible and user-friendly. We believe that reliable public transport information can help reduce traffic congestion, lower pollution levels, and make Bangalore a more livable city.

We work tirelessly to ensure that our data is accurate, comprehensive, and updated regularly. Our goal is to empower commuters with the information they need to make informed decisions about their daily travel needs.

By providing this service, we hope to contribute to a more sustainable urban transport ecosystem and improve the quality of life for all Bangalore residents.
