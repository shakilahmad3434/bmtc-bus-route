Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AMTS and bus route.

Use our Ahmedabad Bus Route Finder tool by entering your source and destination. For live updates, check the AMTS App or Google Maps.

  • AMTS: Standard (non-AC) and mini-buses.
  • BRTS: AC and non-AC buses on dedicated lanes.

Tickets are available at bus stops, and monthly passes can be purchased from the Ahmedabad Municipal Transport office or online.

Popular routes have buses every 10-15 minutes, while other routes may have a frequency of 30-45 minutes.

Limited AMTS services run until 11 PM. BRTS operates until 10 PM.

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport is connected by special BRTS bus services from major parts of the city.
