Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions about Faridabad Bus Services

You can use our Faridabad Bus Route Finder to search for the best bus routes by entering your source and destination. You can also check live updates using the official Transport App or Google Maps.

Faridabad offers various types of buses including regular city buses, low-floor buses, air-conditioned buses, and premium services connecting major destinations.

Tickets can be purchased directly from the conductor on the bus. Monthly passes are available at major bus terminals and transit centers throughout Faridabad.

Multiple bus routes connect Faridabad to Delhi, with frequencies as high as every 20 minutes. You can also use the metro via the Violet Line which connects Faridabad to Delhi.

Most regular services end by 10:30 PM, but limited night service buses operate on select major routes until midnight.
