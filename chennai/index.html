Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MTC bus routes.

You can use our Chennai Bus Route Finder to search for the best MTC bus routes by entering your source and destination. You can also check live updates using the Official MTC App or Google Maps.

MTC offers various bus services including ordinary buses, express services, deluxe air-conditioned buses, and small buses for narrow lanes. Each type serves different routes and passenger needs at varying fare structures.

Tickets can be purchased directly on the bus from conductors. Monthly passes are available at major bus depots including Broadway, T. Nagar, and Adyar. Smart cards can be purchased at designated MTC counters.

Buses on main routes operate every 5-10 minutes during peak hours and every 15-20 minutes during off-peak hours. Routes with less traffic may have buses every 30-45 minutes.

MTC operates night service buses on select routes until midnight. Limited night services operate beyond midnight on major routes like Anna Salai, Mount Road, and routes connecting to Central and Egmore railway stations.

MTC operates dedicated airport services (routes 18A, 54B and 18C) connecting the airport to major locations like Central Railway Station, T. Nagar, and Koyambedu. These air-conditioned buses run at 20-30 minute intervals.
