BMTC Night Bus Service: Timings, Charges, and Routes

Published on 01/03/2025

Finding affordable transport at night in Bangalore can be difficult, as cabs and autos charge higher fares. To solve this problem, the Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC) runs a special Night Bus Service to provide safe and budget-friendly travel across the city, including routes to Kempegowda International Airport.

Key Features of BMTC Night Bus Service

  • Operating Hours: 10:00 PM to 3:30 AM.
  • Number of Buses: Over 200 buses cover different routes.
  • Dedicated Staff: Special night shift drivers and conductors are assigned.
  • Main Hub: Most buses start from Majestic Bus Depot and connect to key areas, including the Airport.

Why Choose BMTC Night Bus Service?

  • Affordable: Much cheaper than taxis and autos.
  • Safe & Reliable: A trusted option for late-night travel.
  • Wide Coverage: Connects major locations like Kadugodi, Attibele, Electronics City, Chandapura, Yeshwanthpur, Yelahanka, Shivajinagar, and the Airport.
  • Convenient for Night Workers: Ideal for professionals working late shifts.
  • Eco-Friendly: Promotes public transport and reduces traffic congestion.

BMTC Night Bus Routes

BMTC operates night buses on carefully planned routes:

  • Main Hub: Majestic Bus Depot
  • Major Stops: Kadugodi, Attibele, Electronics City, Chandapura, Yeshwanthpur, Yelahanka, Shivajinagar, Bangalore Airport, and other key areas.
  • Bus Frequency: Buses run every 15 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the route.

Tip: Use the MyBMTC App to check live bus tracking, routes, and schedules.

BMTC Night Bus Timetable

  • Start Time: Around 10:00 PM from Majestic Bus Depot.
  • End Time: Around 3:30 AM.
  • Frequency: Every 15 minutes on major routes and up to 1 hour on others.

BMTC Night Bus Charges

Fares vary based on distance and routes. Some routes may have slightly higher charges than regular BMTC fares due to lower passenger volume at night.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Are BMTC buses available after 11:00 PM?

Yes, BMTC Night Buses operate from 10:00 PM onwards. Use the MyBMTC App to check live timings.

Q2: What are the operating hours of the BMTC Night Bus Service?

The service runs between 10:00 PM and 3:30 AM.

Q3: How can I find information about routes and timings?

Check the BMTC official website or MyBMTC App for real-time updates.

Q4: Are night bus fares the same as regular fares?

Fares are mostly similar but may be slightly higher on some routes.

Q5: Where can I board the night bus?

At major stops like Majestic, Kadugodi, Yelahanka, and others.

Q6: Is it safe to travel on the night bus?

Yes, BMTC ensures passenger safety. Follow standard travel precautions.

Q7: How do I know when to get off?

Inform the conductor about your stop, and they will assist you.

Q8: Can I use my BMTC bus pass for night buses?

Some passes may be valid; confirm with BMTC officials.

Q9: Where can I give feedback about the service?

Submit feedback through the BMTC website, MyBMTC App, or customer service centers.

Final Thoughts

The BMTC Night Bus Service offers a safe, affordable, and convenient travel option for those moving around Bangalore late at night. Whether you need to reach the airport, return from work, or travel across the city, this service ensures a smooth journey.

Stay Updated: Download the MyBMTC App or visit the official BMTC website for real-time updates on schedules and routes.
