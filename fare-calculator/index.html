Mofussil Fare Chart

The Mofussil Service fare chart, effective from February 25-26, 2020, shows fares for different travel distances called "chargeable stages." It has separate rates for adults, children, and senior citizens. Adult fares start at ₹5.00 for the shortest distance (1S stage) and go up to ₹65.00 for the longest distance (20 stages). Children pay about half of adult fares, while senior citizens get a discount, paying roughly 75% of adult fares.

Nice Road Fare Chart

Adult fares start at ₹5.00 for the first 2 kilometers and rise to ₹40.00 for 66 kilometers. Children and senior citizens get discounts, with their fares capped at ₹20.00 and ₹30.00, respectively. Fares increase at specific distance points rather than stage by stage.
