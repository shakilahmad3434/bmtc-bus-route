Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the bus services and routes in Kolkata.

Use our Kolkata Bus Route Finder to search for routes by entering your source and destination. You can also check live updates via the WBTC Mobile App or Google Maps.

WBTC offers various bus types including regular services, AC buses, non-AC buses, and special services connecting major tourist destinations and transportation hubs.

Tickets can be purchased at bus terminals, designated ticket counters, and through the WBTC mobile app. Monthly passes are available at major bus depots and WBTC offices across Kolkata.

Bus frequency varies by route, with popular routes running every 10-20 minutes during peak hours and 20-40 minutes during off-peak hours.

Yes, WBTC operates night services on select routes until midnight, with limited night buses available on major routes between 12 AM and 5 AM.

WBTC operates dedicated airport services from major points in the city including Esplanade, Howrah Station, and Sealdah Station with comfortable AC buses running at regular intervals.
