Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the BSRTC and bus route.

You can use our Patna Bus Route Finder to search for the best BSRTC bus routes by entering your source and destination. You can also check updates using the Official BSRTC App or Google Maps.

BSRTC operates different types of buses to cater to various passenger needs, including:

  • Ordinary Buses – Affordable and commonly used for daily commuting.
  • Semi-Deluxe Buses – More comfortable with better seating.
  • Deluxe Buses – Air-conditioned buses with premium seating.
  • Volvo/AC Buses – Luxury buses for long-distance travel.
  • Electric Buses – Environment-friendly buses operating on selected routes.
  • Online Booking: Available on the official BSRTC website and apps like RedBus, Paytm, and MakeMyTrip.
  • Bus Terminals: Tickets can be purchased at counters in major bus stands like Mithapur Bus Stand and Gandhi Maidan Bus Stand.
  • Onboard: Passengers can buy tickets directly from the conductor inside the bus.
  • Monthly Passes: Available for frequent travelers at BSRTC offices and select kiosks.
  • Within Patna: City buses run every 10–15 minutes on major routes.
  • Intercity Services: Tickets can be purchased at counters in major bus stands like Mithapur Bus Stand and Gandhi Maidan Bus Stand.
  • Regular BSRTC city buses operate till 10:30 PM.
  • Some intercity and express buses continue services until midnight.
  • Night service is limited, and passengers may need to rely on autos or taxis after late hours.
  • BSRTC operates direct and connecting bus routes to Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport (PAT).
  • Bus Stops Near Airport:
    • Mithapur Bus Stand (6 km away)
    • Gandhi Maidan Bus Stand (8 km away)
  • Alternative Options: If no direct bus is available, take a city bus to Mithapur/Gandhi Maidan and then use an auto-rickshaw or cab to the airport.
