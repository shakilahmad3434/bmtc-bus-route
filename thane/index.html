Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Thane and TMT/MSRTC bus services.

Thane Municipal Transport (TMT) is the public transport wing of the Thane Municipal Corporation, established on February 9, 1989. It operates bus services within Thane city and extends to nearby areas like Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Bhiwandi, Mira-Bhayandar, and Vasai-Virar, providing affordable and accessible travel options.

TMT operates over 100 routes, connecting various parts of Thane and its suburbs, including key routes to Mumbai, Kalwa, Mumbra, and Bhiwandi. Exact numbers may vary, but it covers major residential and commercial areas efficiently.

You can use the "WhereISMyTMTBus" app available on Google Play to track live bus locations, route timings, and estimated arrival times. Alternatively, visit local TMT bus depots (e.g., Wagle Estate) or check online platforms like thaneweb.com for schedules.

TMT offers regular buses, air-conditioned (AC) buses, and recently introduced electric-powered AC mini-buses. Fares for AC buses were reduced in 2023, with a minimum fare of Rs 10 and a maximum of Rs 65, making them competitive with BEST and NMMT services.
